## 6. Responses a preguntas comunes del reviewer

### "Is this app medical?"
```
NO. PEPTIQ Research is an educational reference application for research-grade peptide compounds. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. It is not a medical device. It does not collect or process clinical health records. All information is educational based on published peer-reviewed literature.
```

### "Does the app sell prescription drugs?"
```
NO. PEPTIQ products are research-grade compounds for laboratory research only (Research Use Only · RUO). They are not prescription medications, dietary supplements, cosmetics, or any regulated category. They are not approved by FDA/EMA/COFEPRIS for human or veterinary use. All sales include explicit consent and acknowledgment of RUO status.
```

### "Does the app provide medical advice?"
```
NO. The app explicitly refuses to provide medical advice or personalized dosing. The AI Assistant (SARA) is hardcoded to redirect medical questions to qualified health professionals. All content is educational about published research literature.
```

### "Age restriction?"
```
Age verification mandatory at first launch. Users must declare being 18+ and sign a research-use agreement digitally before accessing any functionality. App rated 17+/Mature.
```

## 8. Apple-specific: In-App Purchase Compliance

⚠️ **CRÍTICO:** Apple cobra 30% de IAP. PEPTIQ vende productos físicos (péptidos) → NO requiere IAP de Apple, puedes usar Stripe/MercadoPago directamente.

**Justificación a Apple si preguntan:**
```
PEPTIQ sells physical research-grade products that ship physically to the buyer's address (cold-chain biological samples). Per Apple Guidelines 3.1.5(a), physical goods/services are exempt from In-App Purchase requirements.
```

## 9. Riesgos de rechazo y mitigación

| Riesgo | Probabilidad | Mitigación |
|---|---|---|
| Apple rechaza por "Medical Device" claim | MEDIA | Categoría Reference/Education + descripción no-médica |
| Apple rechaza por "Drug Reference" | BAJA | Marco RUO explícito + 17+ rating |
| Google rechaza por "Restricted Content" | BAJA | Marco educativo + no enlace a compra directa desde la app store version |
| App rechazo "Misleading claims" | BAJA | Lenguaje strict research, no antes/después |

**Plan B si rechazo Apple/Google:**
- PWA install directo desde Safari/Chrome: `peptiqmx.com/app/` con "Add to Home Screen"
- Distribución alternativa: AltStore (iOS), F-Droid (Android), APK directo
